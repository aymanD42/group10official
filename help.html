How to Use This Website

🔹 Navigation

Use the navigation bar at the top of each page to move between sections of the website: Home, Gallery, Booking, Help, and About.

🔹 Home Page

This page gives an overview of the project, our mission, and provides quick access to other pages.

🔹 Gallery & Data

View images related to peace or our event. A data table is included to organize key information. You can hover over rows to highlight them.

🔹 Booking Page

Fill out the form with your personal details, major, gender, and your reflection on Somalia’s Independence Day. Make sure all required fields are completed before clicking “Submit”.

🔹 About Page

Learn more about our group members, their roles, and contact information related to the project.

🔹 Accessibility & Help

We have used semantic HTML tags, labels, and proper input types to ensure the site is accessible to all users. If you face any issue, please contact your instructor or group member.
